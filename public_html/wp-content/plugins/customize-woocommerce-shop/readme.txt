Search for 'Customize Woocommerce Shop'3. Activate Customize Woocommerce Shop from your Plugins page.4. Click on the Customize Shop API sub-menu link under the Woocommerce Menu5. Click on the provided link to register your free api key and enter your credentials in the provided field within your Plugin6. Click on the Customize Shop sub-menu link under the Woocommerce and configure your desired settings.= From WordPress.org =1. Download Customize Woocommerce Shop.2. Upload the 'Customize Woocommerce Shop' directory to your '/wp-content/plugins/' directory, using your favorite method (ftp, sftp, scp, etc...)3. Activate Customize Woocommerce Shop from your Plugins page. (You will be greeted with a Welcome page.)4. Click on the Customize Shop API sub-menu link under the Woocommerce Menu5. Click on the provided link to register your free api key and enter your credentials in the provided field within your Plugin6.
